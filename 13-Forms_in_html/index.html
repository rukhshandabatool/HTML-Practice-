What Is a Form in HTML? An HTML form is like a digital paper you give to your website visitors so they can fill in information like:
  • Name

  • Email

  • Password

  • Message

* When we press Submit, the form sends the information somewhere to be processed (just like giving your paper to someone at the counter).

* Used to collect data from users.

exp; signup page / login / help requests / contact me

syntax of FORM tag:- <form> </form>
